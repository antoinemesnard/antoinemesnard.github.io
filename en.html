Presentation

I am a doctoral researcher in cryptography, under the supervision of Nicolas Sendrier, within the Cosmiq team at Inria Paris, since November 2024.

Research

My main research interest is code-based public key cryptography, especially

  • Generic decoding
  • Decoding of QC-MDPC codes and analysis of BIKE cryptosystem, a Key Encapsulation Mechanism candidate to the NIST post-quantum standardization.
